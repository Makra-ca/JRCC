Year-Round Community Events

The Jewish Russian Community Centre hosts various events for members of our community of all ages, including cooking demos, community Shabbat dinners, annual Purim parties, community Passover seders, and a variety of concerts and seminars.

If you would like to attend these events but may not have the financial means to buy you or your family members tickets, we may be able to help you!

  • Cooking Demonstrations – Learn new recipes and culinary techniques
  • Community Shabbat Dinners – Share a warm Friday night meal together
  • Purim Parties – Annual celebration with costumes, food, and joy
  • Passover Seders – Community seders welcoming all families
  • Concerts & Seminars – Cultural enrichment and learning opportunities

Community is For Everyone

Don't let cost be a barrier to connection. Our events bring people together, create memories, and strengthen bonds. You belong here!
