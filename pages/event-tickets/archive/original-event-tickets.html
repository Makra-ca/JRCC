Throughout the year, the Jewish Russian Community Centre hosts various events for members of our community of all ages, including cooking demos, community Shabbat dinners, annual Purim parties, community Passover seders, and a variety of concerts and seminars. If you would like to attend these events but may not have the financial means to buy you or your family members tickets, we may be able to help you! 

Please contact us to check whether you are eligible for receiving tickets to our holiday events at a reduced price or for free.
