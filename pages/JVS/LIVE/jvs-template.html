Jewish Vocational Services

Need a job? Looking for a better opportunity?

For over 60 years JVS Toronto has been helping people from all backgrounds and with diverse needs to identify their professional strengths and goals, develop skills and achieve success in school, work and life.

For the last three years, the JRCC has been working with JVS Toronto to bring this valuable service to the JRCC community.

"There are many people in our community who have tremendous skills and know-how, but they might not have the connections they need or the ability to market themselves."

"This service helps people polish how they present themselves to prospective employers, gives them guidance about how they can upgrade their skills, and provides access to government and other resources to help those seeking employment."

Employment Services Available

In partnership with the JRCC, personal appointments are available with JVS professionals every Tuesday at the JRCC, offering:

Job Search Support

Professional guidance to help you find the right opportunities

Resume Writing

Expert help crafting compelling resumes and cover letters

Interview Coaching

Practice and perfect your interview skills with professionals

Job Development

Identify opportunities that match your skills and goals

Group Workshops

Learn employment skills in specialized group settings

Career Counseling

Explore career paths and develop long-term strategies

The JVS at the JRCC initiative is part of the JRCC's continuously evolving commitment to provide increased access to social services to Jews from the former Soviet Union and their families residing in Ontario. To learn more or to volunteer your unique talents or expertise to benefit members of our community, please contact us.
