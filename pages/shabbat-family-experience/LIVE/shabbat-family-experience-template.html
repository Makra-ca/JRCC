Shabbat Family Experience

The Jewish Russian Community Centre is offering a Shabbat Family Experience to anyone interested. In the Shabbat Family Experience, you will be matched with a participating family in our community and will be invited to their home for a traditional Shabbat dinner.

Experience the warmth and tradition of Shabbat with a welcoming family from our community.

If you are interested in participating in our Shabbat Family Experience, please contact us.
