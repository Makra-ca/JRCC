JRCC would like to welcome Jewish newcomers to our community with our newcomer packages! If you are a recent Jewish-Russian immigrant (less than 1 year in Canada), you are eligible to receive our newcomer package. New Jewish immigrants will also receive a walk-through of all the services that are offered in our community – at JRCC and other Jewish community services – that may aid their transition. These meetings occur virtually (e.g., Zoom) or via a phone call until further notice.

If you are interested in receiving our newcomer package, please contact us.
