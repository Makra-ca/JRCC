The Jewish Russian Community Centre has partnered with the Jewish Vocational Service of Metropolitan Toronto (JVS) to provide you with the assistance you need to secure employment. JVS offers one-on-one consultations and workshops that prepare you for successfully seeking and obtaining employment opportunities. Their services help community members build key skills, such as job searching on the internet, constructing resumes and cover letters, and interview skills. 

If you are interested in receiving employment assistance and would like to be referred to JVS services, please contact us.
