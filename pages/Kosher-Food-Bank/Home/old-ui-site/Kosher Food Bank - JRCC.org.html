Nourishing Our Community with Care

The JRCCâs Kosher Food Bank provides nutritious and culturally appropriate food to households in need. We provide regular food deliveries to families who are considered low-income and/or at-risk.

Food insecurity poses a serious risk to physical and mental wellness. Oftentimes, social assistance programs are not enough to ensure that households are able to secure healthy and nutritious food in the right quantities. The JRCC food bank was started as a way to support those who are most vulnerable by bringing them appropriate staple foods directly.
