The Jewish Russian Community Centre offers kitchenware to community members that do not have basic kitchenware in their homes and demonstrate financial need. Eligible clients can receive up to $100 worth of basic kitchenware, such as dishes, cups, kettles, and so on. 

Please contact us to check whether you are eligible for receiving kitchenware assistance.
