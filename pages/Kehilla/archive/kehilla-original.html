Do you want to know how to apply?

Information regarding subsidized housing is available at the JRCC offices at 5987 Bathurst Street on Thursday, July 8th from 10:00 am until 1:00 p.m.

You can ask any housing related questions directly to an experienced subsidized housing professional from the Kehilla Residential Programme.
Please note that we do not have special access to subsidized housing, nor can we circumvent the waiting lists or process.

To make an appointment or for more information call 416.222.7105 or email jrcc@jrcc.org.
