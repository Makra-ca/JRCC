Holocaust Survivor Speaker
Sora Vigorito is one of only 89 pairs of identical twins who were subjected to the cruel medical experiments conducted by Dr. Joseph Mengele in Auschwitz. As a young child, she endured unimaginable loss, trauma, and separation, yet emerged with extraordinary strength and determination.
Her life story is a testament to the human spiritâ€™s capacity for survival, healing, and renewal. Through decades of rebuilding, Sora transformed profound devastation into a life rooted in faith, purpose, and resilience. Her remarkable journey has been documented in an Emmy Award winning film, Sora, which she also directed, capturing both the pain of her past and the courage that defines her present.

Today, Sora Vigorito shares her story to educate, inspire, and ensure that the lessons of history are never forgotten.
